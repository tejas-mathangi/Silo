# AtliQ Corp - Finance Policy 2024
**Confidentiality Level: Manager (L2)**
**Department: Finance**

## 1. Expense Reimbursement
All employees can claim up to $50 for daily meals during travel. Managers can approve expenses up to $500 without further authorization.

## 2. Salary Disclosure
Salary information is strictly confidential. Only Finance Managers (L2) and C-Suite (L3) executives have access to full payroll audits.

## 3. Annual Audit
The annual audit for 2023 was completed by Deloitte. The net profit increased by 15% compared to the previous fiscal year.
